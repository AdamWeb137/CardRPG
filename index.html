Card RPG

A simple party game where the objective is to eliminate your opponents.

Each round, you say what you wish to do, and a card is drawn. If the card is high, your wish is granted. Otherwise, who knows what'll happen to you!

Instructions
  1. Input every players name or character name
  2. Appoint a "narrator" to get the game going
  3. Each player then says what they want to do each round
  4. Draw a card. The higher the cards number, the closer the reality is to your wish. The narrator will input what happens each round.
  5. Make sure each action follows an overarching narrative
  6. Play until only one player remains. Eliminate your opponents!
